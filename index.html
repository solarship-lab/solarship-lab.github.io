Government Subsidy for Solar Installation

The Indian government offers attractive subsidies through the PM-SURYAGHAR scheme to promote solar adoption:

Residential Subsidy

  • ₹30,000 subsidy for 1kW capacity solar systems.
  • ₹60,000 subsidy for 2kW capacity solar systems.
  • ₹78,000 subsidy for systems between 3kW to 10kW.

Additional Benefits

  • No upper cap on system capacity for subsidy.
  • Concessional loans available through nationalized banks.
  • Net metering facility for grid-connected systems.
